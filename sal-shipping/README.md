# Sal's Shipping

Sal’s Shippers is a shipping company offering various shipping options to ensure customers get the best and most affordable service. This project calculates the cost of shipping a package based on its weight using three different shipping methods provided by Sal’s Shippers.

## Shipping Options

### Ground Shipping
Ground Shipping includes a small flat charge plus a rate based on the weight of the package.

**Rates:**
- **2 lb or less**: $1.50 per pound + $20.00 flat charge
- **Over 2 lb but less than or equal to 6 lb**: $3.00 per pound + $20.00 flat charge
- **Over 6 lb but less than or equal to 10 lb**: $4.00 per pound + $20.00 flat charge
- **Over 10 lb**: $4.75 per pound + $20.00 flat charge

### Ground Shipping Premium
Ground Shipping Premium has a higher flat charge but no weight-based charges.

**Flat Charge:**
- $125.00

### Drone Shipping
Drone Shipping has no flat charge, but the rate based on weight is three times the rate of Ground Shipping.

**Rates:**
- **2 lb or less**: $4.50 per pound + $0.00 flat charge
- **Over 2 lb but less than or equal to 6 lb**: $9.00 per pound + $0.00 flat charge
- **Over 6 lb but less than or equal to 10 lb**: $12.00 per pound + $0.00 flat charge
- **Over 10 lb**: $14.25 per pound + $0.00 flat charge
